Hi, I am Xiang Li, a Year 4 (senior) undergrad from Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University in China & the University of Liverpool in the UK.

I will join the DIVA Group of Institute Polytechnique de Paris (Télécom Paris) and the Augmented Perception Lab at the CMU Human-Computer Interaction Institute as a research intern this summer, co-advised by Jan Gugenheimer and David Lindlbauer.
